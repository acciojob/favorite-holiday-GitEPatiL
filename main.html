Goa


Goa is one of the most popular holiday destinations in India, known for its beaches, nightlife, Portuguese heritage, and coastal food culture. It offers a mix of relaxation and adventure with water sports, historic churches, and vibrant markets.

  1. Baga Beach – famous for water sports and nightlife.
  2. Fort Aguada – historic fort with sea views.
  3. Dudhsagar Falls – one of the tallest waterfalls in India.
  4. Basilica of Bom Jesus – UNESCO heritage church.
  5. Anjuna Flea Market – popular shopping and culture spot.
  • Goan Fish Curry
